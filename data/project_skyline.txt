Skyline Tower

Project Name: Skyline Tower
Project ID: ST-2026

Project Overview:
Skyline Tower is a high-rise office and residential building designed to combine modern architecture with premium amenities. The project features mixed-use floors, including commercial offices, residential units, and rooftop recreational spaces with panoramic views.

The design emphasizes sustainability, structural resilience, and luxury finishes to attract high-value tenants and residents.

Scope of Work:
The project involves structural construction, interior fit-outs, façade installation, and integration of building systems. Key tasks include foundation work, steel framework assembly, concrete pouring for core and floors, and installation of large glass panels for exterior façades.

Timeline:
Construction began in early March 2025 following completion of planning and permits.

Start Date: 2025-03-01

The estimated completion date is currently projected for mid-September 2026. Certain finishing works and interior installations may continue slightly beyond this date depending on material delivery and workforce scheduling.

Estimated Completion Date: 2026-09-15

Some internal references also use the phrasing “late 2026” for flexibility in planning.

Materials and Construction:
The main construction materials include concrete, steel, and glass. Concrete is used extensively in structural cores, flooring, and foundations to ensure stability and durability.

Steel forms the primary framework for the tower, supporting both residential and commercial sections. Glass panels are incorporated across façades and windows, providing natural lighting and a modern aesthetic.

Progress and Updates:

Construction experienced delays in Q2 2025 due to supply chain issues affecting concrete and steel deliveries.
To mitigate delays, additional workforce was added in July 2025 to accelerate ongoing construction phases.
Structural framework is now back on schedule, and exterior works are progressing as planned.

Additional Notes:
The project includes energy-efficient systems, automated climate control, and safety monitoring integrated during construction. Rooftop amenities and landscaped communal areas are planned for completion in the final phase.
