Harbor Residential Complex

Project Overview:
The Harbor Residential Complex is a luxury apartment development located near the waterfront, designed to provide high-end living spaces with modern amenities. The project includes multiple residential towers, landscaped communal areas, and recreational facilities such as gyms and rooftop terraces.

The development targets both long-term residents and seasonal tenants, offering a combination of private apartments and shared community spaces to enhance resident experience.

Scope of Work:
Construction involves both structural and finishing works. Structural tasks include erecting residential towers, reinforcing foundations, and integrating utility infrastructure. Finishing works include interior fittings, landscaping, and installation of energy-efficient systems.

Timeline Details:
Construction officially began in mid-February 2025 following planning approvals and site preparation.

Construction began: 2025-02-14

The estimated finish date is projected for October 10, 2026, though minor adjustments may occur depending on material delivery schedules, weather conditions, and tenant customization requests.

Estimated finish: 2026-10-10

Some internal reports also refer to “late 2026” as a target completion window to account for potential schedule shifts.

Materials and Construction:
The project uses a combination of traditional and durable materials suitable for luxury residential construction. Brick is used extensively for exterior façades, providing both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity.

Concrete forms the foundation, structural walls, and flooring systems, ensuring long-term stability. Wood is incorporated in interior finishes, flooring, and decorative elements to provide warmth and style.

Additional Notes:
High demand for units has accelerated certain construction phases, particularly interior finishing and landscaping. Scheduling adjustments have been made to ensure that priority areas are completed on time.

Other considerations include environmental sustainability measures, such as low-emission materials and water-saving systems, which are being gradually integrated into the construction plan.
